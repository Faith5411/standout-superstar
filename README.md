## What is Standout Superstar?

[Standout Superstar](https://standoutsuperstar.com) is a live online talent competition where performers from around the world compete in real-time, audiences vote live, and the best rise to Superstar status. No TV network required. No geography limits. Just pure talent.

**Launching April 10, 2026** at [standoutsuperstar.com](https://standoutsuperstar.com).

---

## How It Works

### The Contestant Journey

1. **Sign Up** — Create your profile at [standoutsuperstar.com/contestant](https://standoutsuperstar.com/contestant) ($25/month subscription)
2. **Submit Videos** — Upload up to 5 performance videos ($75 per video)
3. **Live Voting** — Each video is voted on by the live audience
4. **Approval Threshold** — Videos need **75%+ approval** from voters to count
5. **Earn Superstar Status** — Get 5 approved videos to graduate to **Superstar**
6. **Compete for the Crown** — Every 6 months, all Superstars compete for the **Standout Superstar** title
7. **Permanent Recognition** — The winner is crowned and featured permanently on the [Superstar Hall](https://standoutsuperstar.com/superstar)

### For Viewers

- **Free viewing** of all [live performances](https://standoutsuperstar.com/live)
- **[VIP Access](https://standoutsuperstar.com/vip)** ($9.99/month) for exclusive content, early voting, and behind-the-scenes access
- **Real-time voting** — every viewer's vote counts
- Browse performers by [category](https://standoutsuperstar.com/categories)

## FAQ

See our full [FAQ page](https://standoutsuperstar.com/faq) for answers to common questions.

**Q: How do I become a contestant?**
Visit [standoutsuperstar.com/contestant](https://standoutsuperstar.com/contestant) to sign up.

**Q: What types of talent can I showcase?**
Any performance talent — singing, dance, comedy, music, magic, spoken word, and more. See all [categories](https://standoutsuperstar.com/categories).

**Q: How does voting work?**
Live audiences vote during performances. Videos need 75%+ approval to count toward Superstar status.

**Q: What is Superstar status?**
After getting 5 approved videos, you become a Superstar and compete for the Standout Superstar crown every 6 months.

**Q: Is there a kids division?**
Yes! Visit [standoutsuperstar.com/kids](https://standoutsuperstar.com/kids).
