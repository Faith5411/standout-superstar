# Standout Superstar ⭐

**The world's first live online talent competition.**

🌐 **Website:** [standoutsuperstar.com](https://standoutsuperstar.com)

## What is Standout Superstar?

Standout Superstar is a live online talent show where anyone can perform for 15 minutes and let a global audience vote. No auditions. No judges. No gatekeepers. Just you, your talent, and the world watching.

### 15 Talent Categories
🎤 Singing | 💃 Dance | 😂 Comedy | 🎵 Rap | 🎧 DJ | 🎩 Magic | 👨‍🍳 Cooking | 🎮 Gaming | 💪 Fitness | 🥋 Martial Arts | 📝 Spoken Word | 🎨 Art | 👗 Fashion | 🎭 Acting | ⭐ Wildcard

### How It Works
1. **Sign up** for $25/month at [standoutsuperstar.com/contestant](https://standoutsuperstar.com/contestant)
2. **Submit your performance video** — 15 minutes of your best talent
3. **We air it live** to a global audience who votes in real-time
4. **Rise to the top** — the best performers become Superstars
